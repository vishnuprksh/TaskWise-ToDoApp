Account Deletion Request

We value your privacy and provide a straightforward way to delete your account and associated data from TaskWise.

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent. All your tasks, projects, and time-tracking history will be immediately and irrecoverably removed from our servers.

How to Delete Your Account

To request the deletion of your TaskWise account and all associated data, please send an email to our support team from the email address associated with your account.

Please include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line.

Request Deletion via Email

What Data is Deleted?

  • Your user profile information (Name, Email).
  • All Tasks created by you.
  • All Projects created by you.
  • All Time-tracking logs and history.

Requests are typically processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
