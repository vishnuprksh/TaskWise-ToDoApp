Privacy Policy for TaskWise

Last updated: December 25, 2024

TaskWise ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by TaskWise.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you sign in using Google Authentication, we collect your name and email address to create and manage your account.

App Data: We collect the tasks, projects, and time-tracking data you create within the app to provide the core functionality of the service and synchronize your data across devices.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our app.
  • Synchronize your tasks and projects across multiple devices.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our app.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our app.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore) for cloud synchronization and AsyncStorage for local storage on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, but please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

TaskWise uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore)
  • Expo

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your tasks and projects at any time within the app. If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated data from our servers, please visit our Account Deletion Request page or contact us at the email provided below.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
