Take control of your day with TaskWise, the modern productivity app designed to help you focus on what truly matters. Whether you're managing personal projects, work assignments, or daily shopping lists, TaskWise provides the tools you need to stay organized and efficient.

TaskWise isn't just another to-do list. It’s a comprehensive productivity system that helps you prioritize tasks based on their real impact.

**Key Features:**

*   **Smart Priority Scoring:** Stop guessing what to do next. TaskWise uses a unique scoring system based on Easiness, Importance, Emergency, and Interest to help you identify high-value tasks at a glance.
*   **Integrated Time Tracking:** Understand where your time goes. Track the duration of every task with a single tap and view total time spent across projects.
*   **Project-Based Organization:** Keep your life categorized. Create custom projects with vibrant colors to separate work, home, and hobbies.
*   **Home Screen Widget:** Stay productive without even opening the app. Our sleek Android widget lets you view your tasks and switch between projects directly from your home screen.
*   **Seamless Cloud Sync:** Never lose your data. Sign in with Google to securely sync your tasks and projects across all your Android devices using Firebase.
*   **Premium Modern Design:** Enjoy a beautiful, intuitive interface with full dark mode support, smooth animations, and a clutter-free experience.
*   **Quick Interactions:** Use intuitive gestures like swipe-to-delete and click-to-edit to manage your list with lightning speed.
